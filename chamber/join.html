Membership Levels

Non-Profit

Yearly Cost = none

All local non-profit businesses get the same access as a silver level member without any associated cost. Non-profit documentation must be provided before benefits can begin and must be provided yearly.

Bronze Membership

Yearly Cost = $250

  • Business listed in yearly 'Businesses of Kaitaia' booklet
  • Twice yearly shoutouts through social media (Facebook, Twitter, etc)
  • Monthly networking events for all Chamber members

Silver Membership

Yearly Cost = $500

  • All Bronze level benefits
  • Quarterly social media shoutouts
  • Twice yearly feature on our website
  • Limited access to Chamber Business Support Services
  • Access to monthly seminars, workshops, and trainings

Gold Membership

Yearly Cost = $1000

  • All Silver level benefits
  • Monthly social media shoutouts
  • Quarterly feature on our website
  • Full access to Chamber Business Support Services
  • Become part of the Chamber Discounts and Savings Group
