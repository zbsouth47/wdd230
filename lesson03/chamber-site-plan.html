Kaitaia Chamber of Commerce

This is website for the Chamber of Commerce for Kaitaia, New Zealand

Site Purpose

This website serves to promote the businesses of Kaitaia and also to link the citizens of Kaitaia to the businesses of Kaitaia.

Scenarios

  • What resources do we offer for Local Businesses?
  • What can local businesses offer to the people of Kaitaia?
  • What is the Kaitaia Chamber of Commerce doing to help local businesses?
